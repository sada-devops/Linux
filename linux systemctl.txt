Root previlege is recommended

systemctl poweroff > switch off the system/server

systemctl shutdown > switch off the system/server
    systemctl shutdown -h now (without notification)
    systemctl shutdown -h +10 (10 min from now, sends notification)
    systemctl shutdown -c (to cancel the shutdown, sends notification)
    systemctl shutdown -h +10 "notification txt"
    systemctl shutdown -h 22:15(24hr format )
    systemctl shutdown -r (shutdown, reboot)
    systemctl shutdown -H (halt)

systemctl reboot > to reboot the system/server

systemctl halt > stop the server without shutting down

sleep mode > suspend, hibernate, hybrid-sleep, suspend-then-hibernate
    systemctl suspend (session will be stored in RAM, halts system)
    systemctl hibernate (session will be stored in disk, press powerbutton to wakeup)
    systemctl hybrid-sleep (session stored in RAM and disk, powerbutton to wakeup)
    systemctl suspend-then-hibernate (suspend and then hibernate)

schedule to reboot - cron jobs(scheduler)
